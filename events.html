MR - Rallul

 

Channelled Summon x2

During this Event Phase, all Stone Golems you control are considered to be Walls. You may Summon Mercenary Units during this Event Phase.

 

Fury of Godshome x1

One at a time, remove up to 3 wounds from each Wall in play and add them to a Common or Champion Unit that is adjacent to that Wall.

 

Magic Pulse x2

Place any number of cards from your hand on top of your Magic Pile. Add that number to your Summoner's Attack Value until the end of this turn.

 

Reform Stone x2

Take 1 Wall or Stone Golem from any Discard Pile and place it in any empty space on your side of the Battlefield. You control that card.

 

Wall Transference x2

Choose a Wall that you control. You may place the chosen Wall on any empty space on your side of the Battlefield.

 

 

SE - Selundar

 

Into Darkness x2

Choose an opponent. If you have fewer Units on the Battlefield than that opponent does, choose up to 2 of that opponent's Common Units that have a Summon Cost of 2 or less. Discard the chosen Units.

 

Shadows x3

Instead of playing this card during your Event Phase, you may play this card when a Common or Champion Shadow Elf Unit that you control is destroyed and placed in an opponent's Magic Pile. Remove the destroyed Unit from your opponent's Magic Pile and place it face down on top of your Magic Pile.

 

Summon the Night x2

Choose an opponent. During the chosen opponent's next Movement Phase, that opponent's Units can only move up to 1 space each. During the chosen opponent's next Attack Phase, that opponent's Units cannot attack cards they aren't adjacent to.

 

Stalking Advance x2

You may move all Shadow Elf Units that you control up to 1 space each.

 

 

 

SG - Krusk

 

Duck and Cover x3

All Sand Goblin Units you control gain the following ability until the beginning of your next turn:

CAMOUFLAGE

This Sand Goblin cannot be attacked by non-adjacent Units.

 

Mirage x1

One at a time, you may place each Wall that you control on any empty space on your side of the Battlefield. Remove up to 3 Wound markers from each Wall placed in this way. You may Summon Units during this Event Phase.

 

Taunt x3

Choose up to 2 Common enemy Units. One at a time, place each of the chosen Units adjacent to a Sand Goblin you control that is within 3 spaces of the chosen Unit.

 

Shiny x2

All Units you control that have the Scavenge ability gain the following ability until the end of this turn:

STOLEN WEAPONS

Add 2 to this Unit’s Attack Value if there are 1 or more cards under this Unit.

 

 

SO - Mugglug

 

Ambush x2

Swamp Orcs that you control gain the following Special Ability until the end of this turn:

VINEMANCER AMBUSH

If this Unit moves onto or off of 1 or more Vine Wall spaces during this turn, add 1 to its Attack Value until the end of this turn. This Unit does not have to roll to move off of a Vine Wall.

 

Ensnare x2

Choose up to 2 Common Units. One at a time, place each of the chosen Units onto an unoccupied Vine Wall that is within 4 spaces of that Unit.

 

Vine Growth x3

Choose a Wall that you control. Place a Vine Wall adjacent to the chosen Wall.

 

Vine Guard x2

Swamp Orcs that you control gain the following Special Ability until the beginning of your next turn:

VINEMANCER GUARD

When an opponent rolls to attack this Swamp Orc, if this Swamp Orc is adjacent to a Vine Wall, it only receives Wound Markers from die results of 5 or higher during that attack.

 

DD - Tundle

 

Illusionary Warrior x2

Choose a common Unit that is in your Magic Pile. Place the chosen Unit adjacent to a Deep Dwarf Champion or Gem Mage that you control. You control the chosen Unit.

 

Magic Stasis x2

Choose an opponent. Until the beginning of your next turn, the chosen opponent cannot spend Magic Points.

 

Magic Torrent x1

You may spend 1 or more Magic Points. For each Magic Point spent you may place 1 Wound Marker on a Common or Champion enemy Unit that is within 4 spaces of your Summoner. No Unit can receive more than 1 Wound Marker from this Event.

 

Summoning Surge x2

Choose an Opponent. If you have fewer Units on the Battlefield that that opponent does, you may remove up to 3 cards from your Discard Pile and place them on top of your Magic Pile. You may Summon Units during this Event Phase.

 

Wake the Father Gem x2

Until the end of this turn, all special abilities that cost 1 or more Magic Points to activate cost 1 less Magic Point to activate.

 

 

FL - Demagogue

 

Channel Corruption x2

You may immediately summon a Mutation Card without paying its Summon Cost.

 

Heretic's Rebuke x2

You may take control of an enemy Common Unit within 4 spaces of your Summoner. You must immediately Summon a Mutation card by paying its Summon Cost and placing it on that Common Unit.

 

Possessed Wall x2

Choose an enemy Wall and place this card on top of it. The chosen Wall cannot be used as a summoning point while this card is on top of it. At any time, a player may choose to discard any 2 cards from their hand and discard this card from off the Possessed Wall. If the Possessed Wall is destroyed, discard this card.

 

Shield of the Hopeful x3

Instead of playing this card during your Event Phase, you may play this card when a Filth Common Unit that does not have a Mutation Card on it would receive 1 or more Wound Markers. Ignore all Wound Markers that Unit just received.
